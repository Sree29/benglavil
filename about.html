ABOUT US
Benglavil – Experts in Timeless Interior Courtyard Designs Kerala
At Benglavil, we turn ordinary outdoor and indoor spaces into beautiful, relaxing environments that tell a story. For over eight years, we’ve been creating interior courtyard designs Kerala homeowners admire — spaces that blend creativity, craftsmanship, and care. Our goal is simple: to design landscapes and courtyards that reflect your lifestyle while remaining naturally elegant and long-lasting.

What began as a small idea has now grown into one of Kerala’s most trusted names in landscaping and indoor courtyard design in Kerala.
